THE WONDERS OF MADISON SQUARE PARK

NORTH SIDE

The north side of the park has monuments to Admiral Farragut and President Arthur.

Admiral David Glasgow Farragut

Admiral Farragut had a long career in the U.S. Navy, serving in both the War of 1812 and the Civil War (North).

President Chester Alan Arthur

President Arthur was Vice-President of the United States under Pres. James Garfield. When Pres. Garfield was assassinated, Arthur became the 21st President.



Madison Square Park is located at 23rd-26th Streets between Madison and Broadway Avenues. It is easily accessible by 6/N/R/F/M trains.
