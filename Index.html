THE WONDERS OF MADISON SQUARE PARK

Madison Square Park is a lovely park located between Broadway and Madison Avenues and between 23rd and 26th Streets. It has several winding footpaths, several fountains, a Shake Shack, and multiple monuments to figures and events from American history.


Browse through this website to learn more about the park and its "inhabitants"!



Madison Square Park is located at 23rd-26th Streets between Madison and Broadway Avenues. It is easily accessible by 6/N/R/F/M trains.
